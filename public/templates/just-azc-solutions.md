# Potential Just AZC Solutions for New York Cannabis Industry Challenges

1. **Cash Management and Banking Services**
   - Problem: The report mentions "Cash-Intensive Operations" as a major pain point for dispensaries.
   - Just AZC Solution: Provide secure cash management services and a banking-like platform for cannabis businesses, reducing the risks and inefficiencies associated with cash-heavy operations.

2. **Compliance Tools**
   - Problem: The report highlights "Regulatory Compliance Struggles" as a significant challenge.
   - Just AZC Solution: Offer compliance tools and services to help businesses navigate complex regulatory requirements, potentially integrating with state systems to streamline reporting.

3. **Business Dignity and Professionalism**
   - Problem: The report notes a "Lack of Business Dignity and Professionalism" in the industry.
   - Just AZC Solution: By providing professional financial services, Just AZC could help elevate the status of cannabis businesses, making them more attractive to partners and investors.

4. **Financial Controls**
   - Problem: The report mentions an "Inability to have generally accepted business fiduciary controls".
   - Just AZC Solution: Offer financial management tools and services that implement standard business controls, improving accountability and financial planning.

5. **Secure Transactions**
   - Problem: The report discusses the need for secure in-state transactions.
   - Just AZC Solution: The CHITS marketplace could provide a secure platform for B2B transactions within the cannabis industry.

6. **Community and Networking**
   - Problem: The report mentions the importance of building a strong cannabis business community.
   - Just AZC Solution: The exclusive B2B club aspect of Just AZC could provide networking opportunities and a platform for sharing best practices among cannabis businesses.

7. **Data and Reporting**
   - Problem: The report highlights a "Lack of Data and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)".
   - Just AZC Solution: If Just AZC's platform includes robust data analytics and reporting features, it could help businesses and regulators track important metrics and improve decision-making.

8. **Streamlined Application Process**
   - Problem: The report details a complex and opaque licensing process.
   - Just AZC Solution: While not directly solving this, Just AZC's platform could potentially integrate with state systems to help streamline parts of the application process, especially related to financial documentation and compliance.

9. **Customer Service**
   - Problem: The report notes "Sparse Customer Service" from the regulatory agency.
   - Just AZC Solution: By providing responsive customer service to its users, Just AZC could help fill gaps in support for cannabis businesses.

While Just AZC cannot directly solve all the regulatory and operational challenges faced by New York's cannabis industry, its platform and services could significantly ease many of the financial and operational burdens faced by cannabis businesses, potentially making the overall ecosystem more efficient and compliant.
