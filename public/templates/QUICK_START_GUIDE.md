## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### **IF CASH RUNWAY < 30 DAYS:**
1. STOP all discretionary spending immediately
2. Accelerate ALL receivables collection
3. Defer ALL payables (negotiate with suppliers)
4. Emergency fundraise from founder network
5. Consider emergency layoffs
6. Refer to Template #11: Crisis Response Playbook

### **IF GROSS MARGIN STILL NEGATIVE AFTER 30 DAYS:**
1. Review pricing strategy (are you too cheap?)
2. Consider killing unprofitable SKUs
3. Emergency co-packer negotiation
4. Explore alternative raw material suppliers
5. May need to raise prices even if it hurts volume

### **IF BRIDGE LOAN STALLS:**
1. Expand lender outreach to 20+ prospects
2. Consider less favorable terms (higher interest)
3. Explore convertible note structure
4. Activate board network for warm intros
5. Prepare emergency fundraising deck
